Change to points on money left at the end of the auction
In 2022 1 point was awarded to every 1000 bucks that is left. This will be increased to 10 points
Player retention and price match rules
Every team that renews its membership (i.e same owner and name) from 2022 to 2023 will be give an option to either retain a player or have an option to match price at auction.

Player retention

  • You can retain a player nomination from your 2022 line up at a price fo 18,000 or price you paid last year, which ever is higher
  • Player nomination retained will not be part of the auction and you start your team with the player and amount deducted from your 100,000

Price match

  • If you do not retain any player nomination, you could request for a price match
  • You will have to name the player nomination you would like to price match, prior to the auction
  • These players will be auctioned first (auction starts with them in the order of last year price) and once the bidding is ended, you would be asked if you would like tp purchase the player
  • You should not participate in the bidding process for this player and wait for your turn to be asked to Price match, else the option will be invalidated
Introduction of Game cards and Player cards
A new set of card will be introduced and allowed to purchase as part of the auction. The cards would help you to control the point scoring and some action during the season. Below is how they work

Game cards

  • They will let you nominate a match where points are doubled or tripled based on the card
  • You wonâ€™t be able to use this card on already doubled or tripled games such as Tour, charity and any other nominated games where doubling or tripling is pre-set
  • When you use this card, the points will be doubled or tripled for everyone not just you

Player cards

  • They will let you nominate a player (note, not player category or nomination) whose points are doubled or tripled based on the card
  • For example if you own a player A as a super batman and super all-rounder and someone else owns it as a Super bowler and bowler (they would benefit too)

Pre vs Post match game and player cards

  • Both Game and Player cards come in two flavours. Pre match and post-match
  • For Pre-match you should request the use of the card before 12 PM on the day of the game
  • For Post-match you should request the use of the card before midnight after the game ends
Exact number of cards and types will be published closer to Auction
